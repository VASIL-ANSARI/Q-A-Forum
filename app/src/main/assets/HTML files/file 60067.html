It is not exactly a white border, the image you are setting in your custom view is not of exactly the same size as your MyCanvas view. You will need to scale or crop your image if you want it to take up all the space without leaving any empty spaces around.

Try adding android:scaleType="fitXY" to your MyCanvas in your layout.xml. Your MyCanvas would look like:

<a9msquare.mustache.Views.MyCanvas
    android:id="@+id/my_canvas"
    android:layout_width="match_parent"
    android:layout_height="match_parent"
    android:scaleType="fitXY" />
