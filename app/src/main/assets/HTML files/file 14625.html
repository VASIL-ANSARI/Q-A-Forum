Yes it's possible. In the TextView tag, add right margin (to adjust characters number you want) and add also this code : android:maxLines="2" and android:ellipsize="end"This cut your sentence by adding "..." when the right limit of the textView is reached.

You obtain something like this for your textView : 

<TextView
    android:layout_width="fill_parent"
    android:layout_height="wrap_content"
    android:maxLines="2"
    android:ellipsize="end"
    android:layout_marginRight="30sp" />
