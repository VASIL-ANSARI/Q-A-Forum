Please note that the use of split() is highly discouraged, it's now deprecated. You could use preg_split() or explode().

What you want to do here is to use REGEX to search for the semi-colons and commas that aren't in the middle of the your text. preg_split() should get the job done with the right expression.

See http://ca3.php.net/manual/en/function.preg-split.php
