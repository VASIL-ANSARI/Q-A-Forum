You need the .pdb file that corresponds to the executable. That'll give you the symbols.

There are a couple tricky parts to debugging a release build:

  1. The order of operations may be off due to optimizations
  2. Whole functions/variables/etc. may be optimized away
  3. In particular, the arguments passed into functions may not exist (for example, 'this' may be a register, not a spot in memory). Windbg is pretty good about getting the stack track from that though, including figuring out the arguments.
