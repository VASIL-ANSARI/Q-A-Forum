This is what you need: http://developer.android.com/google/gcm/index.html

It will do exactly what you want.

In simple turns it will work like this: Once the right event triggers on your server, it sends a request to google cloud to notify your users of the event. BroadcastReceivers in your app will get notified by google and you can process the event any way you want it.

P.S. google for some tutorials for GCM, its not that hard to implement at all.
