You have two situations... Either safe mode must be enabled on your php.ini or the open_basedir must be set.

Fixing the issues..

I. Disabling safe mode.

Open your php.ini and search for this line

safe_mode = On

Change that to

safe_mode = Off

II. Open_base dir

Find this line and add a semicolon before it.

;open_basedir =

After doing these changes , save your file and do a restart of your webserver.
