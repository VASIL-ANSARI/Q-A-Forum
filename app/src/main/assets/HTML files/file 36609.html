Stick to a standard timezone like UTC or GMT (or whatever you fancy the most) and tell your users their token will expire on 2014-08-07 13:36:45 UTC.

You could also tell them how many minutes, hours or days the token will last instead of showing them a full date. It's always nicer to read will last 45 minutes. instead of will last until 2014-08-07 13:36:45 UTC. This way, no matter what timezone the user is, 45 minutes will always last 45 minutes.

Now, you can only guess the user's timezone by their IP address but it'll be just that, a guess. They could be behind a proxy, their ISP could not show you their real location, they could be behind a VPN, etc.

When it comes to dates you don't want to guess, you want to stick with a standard timezone.
