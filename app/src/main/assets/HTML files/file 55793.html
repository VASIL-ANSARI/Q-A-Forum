Found some information on OleDb / OleDbConnection resizing form (bug?) which helped me solve the issue.

It is kind of a microsoft issue, but the problem in my case was that i had installed 2007 Office System Driver: Data Connectivity Components from https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/download/details.aspx?id=23734 as it is needed to open connections to access databases on 64bit computers, but just having that causes the resize issue and provides no errors or feedback into the issue, and as i am not directly changing the size anywhere in my form, it becomes really confusing and hard to debug.

However I also found out that also installing Microsoft Access Database Engine 2010 Redistributable (32bit) found at https://www.microsoft.com/en-au/download/details.aspx?id=13255 fixes the resize issue that occurs when opening the database.

Hopefully this helps other people in the future who are also attempting to load and save data to an access database.
