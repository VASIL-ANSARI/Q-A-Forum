Here's how I would do it:

  1. In my fragment's view xml, have a ViewGroup called R.id.container to hold your ImageViews. In onCreateView, save a reference to this container.
  2. Have a separate xml containing just the ImageView. This way, you don't have to do any programmatic layout.

Then in your onClick, or wherever you need to add the ImageView:

ImageView newView = LayoutInflater.from(getActivity).inflate(R.layout.image.xml);
mImageContainer.addView(newView);
// Ta da!
