Replace 

LayoutInflater inflater = ((Activity) context).getLayoutInflater();

with 

LayoutInflater.from(context);

in your UserAdapter.getView() method.

Always look to your log - in most cases it useful to determine crash reason. You've got ClassCastException which is self-explanable I think. You try to cast adapter attribute context to Activity (line 43 of adapter) same time when you create your adapter you passing getActivity().getBaseContext() to adapter.
