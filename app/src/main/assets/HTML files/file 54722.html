To somebody who may stumble on this in the future - MQTT is a fairly simple, hassle free protocol to setup. Issues like this are most likely at the server side.

In my case, it was most likely CloudMQTT's Free Tier connection/usage limitations that caused this issue. After much time spent trying to figure the guts of Paho out, I simply spun up my own Mosquitto Instance using a handy Ansible role and AWS, and things worked out swell like they should have hours ago :-)
