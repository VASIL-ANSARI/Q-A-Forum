There is no way to hide parts of a text box value while still having them in the data.

You could look at using a content editable div instead (these are generally used as the foundation of so-called wysiwyg editors) and convert <br> to <span style="display: none;">&lt;br&gt;</span> for use inside it.

You'd then have to remove the spans when you pulled the data back out of the div though.
