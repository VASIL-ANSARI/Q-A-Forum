Instead of using constants, you should use the language array, which was meant for this purpose. Look at: http://ellislab.com/codeigniter/user-guide/libraries/language.html

Once you create your language files, you load them in your controller, as such:

$this->lang->load('welcome_page', $lang_from_session_or_db);

$lang_from_session_or_db should vary based on the user's language, so you would store this in a session variable, or in the database, depending on your project.

You can then access those vars using:

$this->lang->line('language_key');

Because where you place your variables will vary based on language, you may want to look into the printf family of functions.
