Maybe you could try the same:

  1. Go to https://apps.paypal.com/user/my-account/applications/manage
  2. Click "View History" below the one App causing trouble
  3. Find a reply from PayPal, something like:

    [...] Please send contact information, business website URL and a brief business summary to compliance@paypal.com and aquire the preapproval before resubmit the app for approval. Otherwise, please consider to change your business model and implement a marketplace model which is using PayPal chained payment API with you as the secondary receiver and the seller as the primary receiver. [...]

  4. Follow their instructions and keep your fingers crossed that they will grant the required privileges
