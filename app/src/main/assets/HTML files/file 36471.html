TL;DR: Watch out for some instances of UIScrollView inside your page that has 0 size (width/height).

I think I figured this out.

As part of my complex view there was a UIScrollView (let's call it SV1) and at some point I needed to hide it. To hide that scrollview I set its frame to the CGRectZero.

During leaving the page the superview (V2) of that zero-ed scrollview was being removed from its superview and for some reason that was causing the crash (I verified that by trying to remove V2 manually during viewWillDisappear and I was able to get exact same crash.

To fix it - instead of assigning CGRectZero to the scrollview SV1, I simply removed it from superview V2 at that time (when I needed to hide it). This solve my problem. Hopefully it will help somebody else.
