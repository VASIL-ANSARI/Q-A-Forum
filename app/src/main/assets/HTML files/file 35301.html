The do...while block generates a point in the square with corners (±1, ±1) until the point fits into a circle of radius 1 centered in the origin. The expected number of iterations is the ratio of areas, which is the constant 4 / π, or O(1) in big-O-notation.

As for how the process is related to Gaussian distribution, it's an application of Box-Muller transform. Here is another page with a bit of explanation which also contains your piece of code.
