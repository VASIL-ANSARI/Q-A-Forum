Why Express? The community edition contains the whole unrestricted (in terms of functionality, not licencing) thing for free.

Regarding interpretation. Either come up with your own language (using bison/flex for instance, have a look at an example) or embed an existing interpreted language, such as python or lua (a random example).

I've successfully implemented lua as a replacement for a real GUI in some of my own projects and can recommend it, however if you'd like to do some real symbolic math, you'd probably need to come up with a language of your own.

Also have a look at Parsing math equations regarding parsing math equations.
