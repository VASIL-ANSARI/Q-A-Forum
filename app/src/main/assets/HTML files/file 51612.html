Change your inputs to this:

echo "<p>ID: <input type='text' name='id' value='" . $value['id'] . "'></p>";
echo "<p>Quantity:  <input type='text' size='20' name='qty' value='" . $value['qty'] . "'/></p>";

The id field should ideally be type="hidden", but at least you can see the number for testing.

Then you can use this to update your session:

$id = $_POST['id'];
$qty = $_POST['qty'];
$_SESSION['item'][$id] = $qty;
