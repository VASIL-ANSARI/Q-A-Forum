The arg2 is NOT the ID of your item, take a look at the official documentation to understand what it is !

The arg3 is NOT the ID of your item in the database, but the row ID of the Android ressource (i.e. R.id.xxx), so you can't just use the data given by the parameters of onItemLongClick().

Knowing this, you can figure out that the line helper.deleteName((arg2+1)); is not correct and doesn't mean anything : arg2+1 means absolutely nothing for your database.

A solution (not the best I think), should be to include an invisible field in your item view, storing the actual _id field of your database. Then, when a long press is detected, you get back this value and pass it to helper.deleteName().
