You can replace all . in your regex with [^\s] or \S to exclude whitespaces from being matched. Also, you have several ? patterns, they also allow spaces. Please check if they are necessary.

So, the fixed pattern will look like this:

\b(([a-zA-Z0-9]){5,20}((\\\\=)|( ?([=:]|(->)) ?)|( =\\))((?=[^\s][a-z#{@specialDown}])(?=[^\s]*[A-Z#{@specialUp}])(?=[^\s]+[0-9])(?=[^\s]+[\!\@\#\$\%\^\&\*\(\)\_\+\|\~\-\=\\`\{\}\[\]\:\;\'\<\>\?\,\[^\s]\/]))[^\s]{8,20})\b
