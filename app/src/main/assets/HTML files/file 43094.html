Typically speaking, modifying <option> elements is finnicky and very difficult to do. This is because they are controlled by the OS, rather than the browser. You may get them to do one thing in one browser on one OS, but I can tell you it won't look the same in a different browser or on a different OS.

Some things you just flat out cannot change.

The only good solution is to use a library JS-based select box, or make one of your own.
