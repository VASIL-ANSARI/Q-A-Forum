Right now you are getting location updates from your activity but when your phone goes to sleep your activity will be stopped and hence the updates will be stopped.

You need to use the PendingIntent version of the requestLocationUpdates() method. Check this link.

This method is suited for the background use cases, more specifically for receiving location updates, even when the app has been killed by the system.

You'll have to make an IntentService or a BroadcastReceiver to keep getting the location updates.
