I was able to solve the problem. I mostly copied the association proxy tutorial from the official doc Here are some pitfalls and tips for other people who struggle:

  • Write your association class after the classes you want to join. (Obvious for most of us, but it doesn't hurt to check)
  • Add an __init__ function to your association class!
  • Double check your backrefs, class and table names. I've myself not looked through when to use which keyword, but most of the time there is your mistake.
