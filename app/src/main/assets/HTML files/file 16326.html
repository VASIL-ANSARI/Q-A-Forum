Aren't you by any chance saving the image in the UI thread, just right during your onPause() call? If yes, you should move the image saving part into the AsyncTask and provide some kind of boolean flag or mutex to prevent similar AsyncTasks from being started while the previous image is still being saved or when it's already saved.

Be careful with using contexts and internal class data just in case your AsyncTask outlives your other classes and make sure it does not touch anything that does not belong to it.
