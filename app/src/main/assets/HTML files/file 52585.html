Try to replace in your web.xml : 

<context-param>
        <param-name>contextConfigLocation</param-name>
        <param-value>
            /WEB-INF/spring-servlet.xml
        </param-value>
    </context-param>

By : 

<context-param>
        <param-name>contextConfigLocation</param-name>
        <param-value>
            classpath:spring-servlet.xml
        </param-value>
    </context-param>
