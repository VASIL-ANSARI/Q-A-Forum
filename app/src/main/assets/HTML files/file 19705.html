You have error on this line:

$cmd = $ffmpeg . " -i " . $src . " -vsync 0 -vf select='not(mod(n,100))' " .  $out . ".jpg";

Change it to:

$cmd = "ffmpeg -i " . $src . " -vsync 0 -vf \"select='not(mod(n,100))'\" " .  $out . ".jpg";

Or try:

$cmd = "ffmpeg -i " . $src . " -vsync 0 -vf select='not(mod(n,100))' " .  $out . ".jpg";

Also when you call exec function call it like this:

exec($cmd,$out);
print_r($out);

Print_r will print you output of what was executed in exec()...
