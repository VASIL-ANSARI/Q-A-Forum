It is rendering each time because you told it to do just that in your each block. This in and of itself is not liked the cause of your problem.

Your problem most likely revolves around your "take_comments_for". First, you are calling it twice, once for the if and once to actually display it.

Second, the way you are loading the relations you are hitting an n+1 issue. You should setup your relationships and your subsequent queries such that you are eagerly loading comments and articles. This will load up the required data up front in 2 calls instead if hitting the DB for every individual item in your each block.
