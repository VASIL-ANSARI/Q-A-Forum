If you are working with sequences or even more generally speaking, bioinformatics, you should definitely have a look at Bioperl. It comes with interfaces for most commonly used data formats in bioinformatics and even with interfaces to certain online ressources, including databases. And then there are Perl modules to interface with databases in general, for SQL databases I would guess that SQL::DB is probably a good starting point, but haven't used any of that myself, so far.
