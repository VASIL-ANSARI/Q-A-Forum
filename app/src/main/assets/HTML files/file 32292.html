There are simple auto layout feature which help you to make your view like you desire.

For the view on the left, choose from the Editor\Pin menu: Top Space to Superview Leading Space to Superview For the view on the right, choose: Top Space to Superview Trailing Space to Superview And for the big view at the bottom: Leading Space to Superview Trailing Space to Superview Bottom Space to Superview
