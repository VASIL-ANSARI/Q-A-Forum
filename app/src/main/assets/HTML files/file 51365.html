For storing your value from edit text to listview on button click, go through the following step on your button click:

  1. get the text from editText and store in a String.

        String myText=editText.getText().toString().trim();

  2. then save it to your arraylist(data structure which you will use to feed data to your listview via your BaseAdapter).

          ArrayList<String> stockList = new ArrayList<String>();
        stockList.add(myText);
  3. mainly this arraylist will have your data of edittext and then you have call adapter.notifydatasetchanged();
